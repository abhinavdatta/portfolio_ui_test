Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit the personal portfolio website of Abhinav Datta.

1. Information We Collect

This is a static portfolio website. We do not actively collect, store, or process personal data. We do not use contact forms, account logins, or data submission tools.

2. Automatically Collected Information

When you visit the site, the hosting provider may automatically record standard server logs, which may include:

  • Your Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • Your browser type and version
  • The pages you visited and the time of your visit
  • The referring site that linked you here

We do not have access to or control over those server logs. They are managed by the hosting provider.

3. Third-Party Links & Embedded Content

The site contains links and embedded content from external platforms, including GitHub, LinkedIn, X/Twitter, GitHub contribution charts, and GitHub summary cards.

Clicking external links will take you to third-party sites. Their privacy practices are controlled by those platforms.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies, web beacons, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics such as Google Analytics.

5. Resume Viewer

The resume button opens a PDF from the same site origin. No personal data is collected or tracked when you open or download the resume.

6. Your Rights

Since this site does not collect or store personal data directly, there is no visitor data to access, modify, or delete from this portfolio.

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if the site functionality changes. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
