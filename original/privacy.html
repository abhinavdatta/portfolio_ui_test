Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit the personal portfolio website of Abhinav Datta (the "Site").

1. Information We Collect

This is a static portfolio website. We do not actively collect, store, or process any personal data. We do not use contact forms, account logins, or data submission tools on this Site.

2. Automatically Collected Information

When you visit the Site, our hosting provider (such as GitHub Pages, Vercel, or Netlify) may automatically record standard server logs, which may include:

  • Your Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • Your browser type and version
  • The pages you visited and the time of your visit
  • The referring site that linked you to the Site

We do not have access to or control over these server logs. They are managed solely by the hosting provider.

3. Third-Party Links & Embedded Content

The Site contains links and embedded content from external platforms. Specifically:

  • GitHub Profile Chart: The Site embeds a dynamically generated contribution graph via ghchart.rshah.org. This service fetches public data from GitHub's API to render the chart image. No personal visitor data is collected by this embed.
  • Social Platforms: GitHub (github.com/abhinavdatta), LinkedIn (linkedin.com/in/abhinav-datta-kaly), and X / Twitter (x.com/NeoTactix).

Clicking these links will take you to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external platforms. We encourage you to read their respective privacy policies.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies, web beacons, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics (such as Google Analytics) on this Site. The embedded GitHub chart does not set cookies in your browser.

5. Resume Viewer

The Site provides a button to view a resume PDF in an embedded modal viewer. This is a direct file served from the same origin as the website. No personal data is collected or tracked when you open or download the resume.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. If you have concerns about data held by our hosting provider or the third-party linked sites, you should contact them directly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the Site's functionality. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
